With over five years experience in journalism, I have covered culture, business and technology in the U.S. and China. For my master's degree at Medill, I covered business stories in Chicago, including earnings, corporate outlook, economic indicators, trend stories and features.

Before coming to the U.S., I worked as a data journalist intern in Shanghai at CBNweekly, China's biggest business magazine. Working at the data journalism lab "The Rising Lab", I applied technical skills including data mining, analytics and visualization to report data-driven business stories in various industries. I also interned at the China bureaus at The Wall Street Journal and Reuters.

Currently I'm open for freelance writer/reporter opportunities in English and Chinese.
